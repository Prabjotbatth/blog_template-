what are some types of riddles?

Certainly! Riddles come in various forms, each with its own unique twist. Here are some common types of riddles:

Ther is some riddles

1.Enigma Riddle:

I speak without a mouth and hear without ears. I have no body, but I come alive with the wind. What am I?
Answer: An echo.

2.Conundrum Riddle:
What has keys but can’t open locks?
Answer: A piano.

3.Red Herring Riddle:
The more you take, the more you leave behind. What am I?
Answer: Footsteps.

4.Situational Riddle:
You see a house with green walls, red curtains, and blue furniture. What color are the stairs?
Answer: There are no stairs; it’s a one-story house!

5.Logic Riddle:
If you have me, you want to share me. If you share me, you no longer have me. What am I?
Answer: A secret.
